HOW EPIMELEIA WORKS

Epimeleia is a protocol that captures today's image and compares it over time. Subscribe and the system handles everything automatically.

The year is divided into 4 quarters — Q1/4 — mirroring corporate balance sheets. A satellite passes over your asset's coordinates between 6 and 7 times per quarter. Two positive results within the observation window are sufficient to certify.

More certifications mean greater credibility — corporate and reputational — before competitors, governments, banks and investors.

NATURE OF THE SERVICE

Epimeleia acts as a digital notary of corporate environmental conduct. It is not an auditor, not a regulatory body, and does not act on behalf of any government or regulatory entity. It is a neutral protocol that records satellite data permanently and immutably on the Polygon blockchain.

PERMANENCE AND IRREVOCABILITY

Every record written to the Polygon blockchain is permanent and immutable. There is no deletion mechanism. The history of certifications, opacity gaps, and excellence seals cannot be modified under any circumstances.

DATA RESPONSIBILITY

The responsibility for the accuracy of declared data rests 100% with the registering organization. If declared data does not match satellite observations, the record reflects this permanently.

MEMBERSHIP AND PRICING

Membership is monthly. The protocol is currently in a testing phase with complimentary access. Prices may change without prior notice.

JURISDICTION

Transactions are executed on Polygon Mainnet (Chain ID: 137). They are irreversible by the nature of blockchain technology.

PRIVACY POLICY
Privacy

PUBLIC DATA ON BLOCKCHAIN

The certification status and validation level of each asset are publicly visible on the Polygon blockchain. Anyone can verify them on Blockscout.

PRIVATE DATA

The following data is strictly private: GPS coordinates, corporate email, legal name, membership balance, payment history, and contact details.

CORPORATE EMAIL

The corporate email is stored only as a cryptographic hash on-chain. The actual email is never recorded on the blockchain.

CONVERSATION DATA

Conversations with agent EPI are processed by the Anthropic API under their own privacy terms. Epimeleia does not store conversation content between sessions.

NO DATA SALES

Epimeleia does not sell, transfer, or commercialize client data to third parties under any circumstances.
